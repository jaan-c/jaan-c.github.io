About

I’m Jaan, a self taught programmer. At the moment I’m trying to get into app development by learning native Android and from time to time I code in Rust.

I got into coding after I got curious about Linux from the show Mr. Robot 6 years ago. The first months, I’ve probably distro-hopped more than my fingers could count. At the moment I’m a happy user of KDE Neon.

This is my blog. For now I don’t have any specific topics I’m planning to write about and I’ve mainly created a blog for three reasons. The first one is that I wanted to take on the 100 Days to Offload challenge I keep reading in Fosstodon, the second is that I’m in desperate need of another hobby besides coding. And the third is to incentivize myself to learn more and have something interesting to share.

Toot me at @bredlof if you have anything to share!
